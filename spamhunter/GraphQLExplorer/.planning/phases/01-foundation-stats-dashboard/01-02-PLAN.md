| T-01-05 | Denial of Service (self-inflicted) | `readiness.ts` backoff loop | mitigate | Bounded backoff (500ms → 5000ms cap); do not poll `/ready` aggressively (contract § 2, Pitfall 8). (V7) |
| T-01-06 | Tampering | opaque cursor handling in `paginate.ts` | mitigate | Treat `endCursor` as opaque — never parse or construct; `INVALID_CURSOR` restarts from page 1. Scaffold only this phase. (V5) |

No `high`-severity threats: read-only API, no user-supplied input rendered, classifier suppresses internal leakage.
