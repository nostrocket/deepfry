<objective>
Build the browser side of the bridge: `GoBridgeTransport`, a third `GraphTransport` implementation
that fetches the binary frame produced by Plan 01, decodes it incrementally with fetch +
ReadableStream into SoA typed arrays with ZERO `JSON.parse` (D-08), and resolves a `GraphBuffers`
extended additively (D-04) with out-degree, community, and the two activity timestamps. Route the
bridge through Vite's `server.proxy` so the fetch is same-origin and the COEP/CORP wall is dodged.

Purpose: This is the drop-in swap (PERF-01) that replaces the JSON-parse wall behind the unchanged
GraphTransport seam — cosmos.gl + the SoA render/layout pipeline stay untouched.
Output: A selectable `?transport=bridge` data path that loads the real graph from the Go bridge;
extended GraphBuffers; a wireFormat module shared as the single source of frame-layout truth;
round-trip decode unit tests.
</objective>
