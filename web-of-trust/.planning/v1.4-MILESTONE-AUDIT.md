## Tech Debt (non-blocking, accepted)

Both items below are quality refinements surfaced by the adversarial review's third iteration. Neither can reintroduce the hang or an unbounded leak.

1. **WR-01 — self-healing cross-batch marker race.** A leftover goroutine setting `completedThisBatch=true` after the next batch resets it can defer one stuck relay's close by a single batch. Self-correcting. Fix: replace the boolean with a per-batch generation token.
2. **IN-02 — quorum-path test coverage gap.** The quorum-cancel connection-close branch lacks a dedicated unit test (the seam doesn't exercise `rs.conn`/atomics).

Recommended follow-up: a small Phase in a future milestone, or a `/gsd-quick` task, to (a) convert the marker to a generation token and (b) add a quorum-path close test. Not required for v1.4's definition of done.

All requirements satisfied, no blockers, no broken flows. Accumulated tech debt is 2 minor, non-blocking, self-healing/coverage items. Safe to complete the milestone; the debt is tracked here for a future pass.
